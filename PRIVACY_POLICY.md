# Privacy Policy

**SonarrSchedule**
Last updated: 19 April 2026

---

## Overview

SonarrSchedule is a tvOS application that connects to your personal Sonarr media server to display your TV schedule and manage your library. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains clearly what data the app collects, how it is used, and what is never collected.

---

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

SonarrSchedule stores the following information in your Apple TV's local storage (UserDefaults):

- **Sonarr server IP address** — the local network address of your Sonarr server
- **Sonarr server port** — the port used to connect to your Sonarr server
- **Sonarr API key** — used to authenticate requests to your Sonarr server

This data is stored solely to maintain your connection settings between app launches. It never leaves your device except to communicate directly with your Sonarr server.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

SonarrSchedule does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

- Personal information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Crash reports sent to third parties
- Advertising identifiers
- Browsing or viewing history
- Any data from your Sonarr library beyond what is displayed on screen

---

## Network Communication

All network requests made by SonarrSchedule are directed exclusively to **your own Sonarr server** on your local network. Specifically:

- The app fetches your TV schedule via your Sonarr iCal feed
- The app queries the Sonarr REST API to display your library and manage shows

No data is sent to the app developer, Apple (beyond standard App Store operation), or any third-party service. The app has no backend server of its own.

---

## Third-Party Services

SonarrSchedule does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

---

## Children's Privacy

SonarrSchedule does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user, regardless of age.

---

## Data Security

Because all data remains on your device and your local network, you are in full control of its security. We recommend:

- Keeping your Sonarr API key confidential
- Ensuring your home network is appropriately secured
- Using Sonarr's built-in authentication features

---
